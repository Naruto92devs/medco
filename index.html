The MedCo App

The MedCo APP grants patients and healthcare providers access to the MedCo ecosystem. Once you are logged into the MedCo APP, you can manage everything from booking your first consultation appointment, and getting a treatment prescription to making payment for healthcare services.

MedCo application will become the central platform to cater to all customer need(s). Asides from managing payments, the app will manage all the end-to-end activities associated with each healthcare provider. The following are some of the features that emergency applications will incorporate:

  • Book Appointment
  • Medicines
  • Insurance
  • Surgeries
  • Lab Tests
